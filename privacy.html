Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

StarPic ("we", "us") is a pre-launch mobile app. This page explains what information we collect when you sign up for early access, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for early access on this page, we collect the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Name
  • Country
  • Email address

We also store a simple click counter in your browser's local storage to understand interest in the "Download App" button. This counter is not linked to your identity and never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To notify you when StarPic launches on the App Store and Google Play.
  • To understand, in aggregate, interest in the app before launch.
  • To occasionally send early-access updates about the app.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Sign-up data is submitted directly to Google Forms and stored in a Google Sheet under our account. Google acts as our data processor for this purpose — see Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles data on its infrastructure.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (EU/UK visitors)

We process your data based on your consent, given when you voluntarily submit the sign-up form.

5. Data Retention

We keep your sign-up information until StarPic launches and related early-access communications are complete, or until you ask us to delete it — whichever comes first.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete it.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@starpic.app.

7. Children's Privacy

StarPic is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Security

We rely on Google's infrastructure to store sign-up data and take reasonable steps to protect it, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as StarPic moves from pre-launch to a live app. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@starpic.app.
